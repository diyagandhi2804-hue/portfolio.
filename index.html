ABOUT ME

I've always been drawn to visuals-how simple ideas can be transformed into something meaningful through design. This curiosity led me to explore graphic design as a way to express, communicate, and create.

I enjoy working on branding and digital creatives, focusing on clean, thoughtful visuals that balance aesthetics with purpose. I'm constantly exploring, learning, and experimenting to refine my style and understand what makes design truly connect with people.

At this stage, I'm focused on building my craft and creating work that is not just visually appealing, but intentional and impactful.
